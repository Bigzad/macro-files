1. Introduction

Welcome to the AI-Powered Macro Calculator ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using the App, you embark on a journey where your personal data may intertwine with our systems. This document orchestrates the relationship between you and us, ensuring transparency and safeguarding your trust.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with captivating nutritional insights and to improve the App's functionality. This may include:

1. Personal Input Data

  • Name, email, height, weight, age, gender, activity level, and dietary goals.
  • Daily meals or victuals you log, including calories and macros.
  • Progress data (e.g., weight tracking, macro adherence).

2. Technical Data

  • Device type, browser, operating system.
  • IP address (for security and troubleshooting).
  • Usage statistics to understand how you navigate our kaleidoscopic features.

3. Optional Feedback

  • When you provide feedback, surveys, or support requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used in ways that orchestrate a seamless and reimagined user experience:

  • To calculate your macros, meal plans, and progress dashboards.
  • To improve and refine our App features, ensuring the tapestry of tools evolves into something more intricate and transcendent.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your information. Your data remains within our verdant ecosystem, focused solely on enhancing your health journey.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (EU & UK GDPR)

For users in the European Union and United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: When you enter personal information, you consent to its processing.
  • Legitimate Interest: To analyze anonymized usage data and improve the App.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required by applicable law.

5. Data Storage and Security

We use secure databases to store your information. Security measures are designed to weave an intricate shield around your data, including encryption, access controls, and monitoring.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely immune from the enigmatic crucible of risk. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Personal input data (e.g., name, email, height, weight, goals) is retained as long as you use the App.
  • Usage data may be stored in anonymized form indefinitely, to help us continually reimagine and improve our features.
  • You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

7. Sharing of Information

We may share data in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the App.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, regulation, or court order.
  • Anonymized Research: Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be used for analysis or research.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA (California), or similar laws. These rights may include:

  • Right to access your data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to request deletion ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to restrict or object to processing.
  • Right to data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@macromasterai.com

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 16 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred across borders (e.g., from the EU to the United States or Canada). We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses, to maintain compliance with global data protection standards.

11. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as laws evolve or as our App grows into a more intricate mosaic of features. Updates will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
